About the Firm

Welcome to
Vectorium Engineering

Vectorium Engineering is a precision mechanical engineering firm based in Pune, Maharashtra, delivering manufacturing-ready designs, structural systems, and complete technical documentation for industrial clients across India and worldwide.

Founded on real-world aerospace and industrial experience, our engineering approach prioritizes manufacturability, cost efficiency, and production scalability — ensuring every file we deliver goes straight to the shop floor without costly rework.

We specialize in complex aerospace component documentation, PMI/MBD workflows, GD&T per ASME Y14.5, and DFM-driven design — the same standards used in global aerospace supply chains.
